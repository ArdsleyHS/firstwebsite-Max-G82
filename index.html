Plant Danger level Picture Description
Poison IvyMildContact with the any part of this plant causes irritation of the skin. Can be more severe for those allergic.
Manchineel TreeExtremeAlso known as the little apple of death, this tropical tree is EXTREMELY dangerous and can be fatal. The fruits of the tree are unsafe to eat, and the bark is covered in a milky sap that causes painful blistering of the skin. Burning this plant creates a toxic smoke that can cause blindness or death.
HogweedHighThis carrot-like plant can grow up to 16ft tall and has a sap that causes skin to blister when exposed to sunlight.
Stinging NettleLowThe sharp little hairs on this plant get caught in the skin of those who come in contact with it. These hairs release a variety of irritants that cause rash, though most symptoms are gone within 24 hours.
